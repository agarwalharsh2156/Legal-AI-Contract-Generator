SERVICE AGREEMENT TEMPLATE

This template provides the structure for generating professional service agreements.
Placeholder variables for customization:

{client} - Client company name
{provider} - Service provider name
{services} - Description of services
{payment} - Compensation terms
{duration} - Contract duration
{date} - Effective date
{jurisdiction} - Governing law

ESSENTIAL SECTIONS:
1. Parties and Recitals
2. Scope of Services
3. Compensation and Payment Terms
4. Term and Termination
5. Intellectual Property Rights
6. Confidentiality
7. Warranties and Representations
8. Limitation of Liability
9. Indemnification
10. General Provisions
11. Signature Blocks

KEY CONSIDERATIONS:
- Clear service deliverables
- Payment schedules and late fees
- IP ownership and licensing
- Liability limitations and caps
- Termination procedures
- Dispute resolution mechanisms

This template ensures comprehensive coverage of all critical
service agreement provisions while maintaining flexibility
for various service types and industries.
