MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT TEMPLATE

This template serves as a baseline for generating comprehensive NDAs.
The following placeholders will be replaced by the contract generator:

{party1} - First party name
{party2} - Second party name
{date} - Effective date
{term} - Agreement term/duration
{jurisdiction} - Governing law jurisdiction

STANDARD SECTIONS TO INCLUDE:
1. Parties and Effective Date
2. Definition of Confidential Information
3. Obligations of Receiving Party
4. Permitted Disclosures
5. Return of Materials
6. Term and Termination
7. Remedies
8. General Provisions
9. Signature Blocks

CRITICAL CLAUSES:
- Broad definition of confidential information
- Reasonable care standard
- Injunctive relief provisions
- Survival of obligations
- Governing law and jurisdiction

This template is used by the AI to generate consistent, comprehensive NDAs
while ensuring all critical legal protections are included.
